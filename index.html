About Us

Multilingual Tourist Helpline Support Center is an initiative led by the Department of Tourism, Nepal to further enhance and streamline tourism in Nepal. Our Support Center offers Multilingual Helpline service dedicated to make your stay in Nepal hassle-free. Think about traveling in a foreign land; whether that be for business or pleasure; you desperately need help and you don’t get it properly just because of the language barrier. You don’t speak the local dialect and they don’t understand your language. It can be frustrating at least and could be life endangering at worse. Imagine yourself in a difficult situation and the help can’t reach you just because the both of you don’t speak the same language. So, in order to mitigate such problems and to make your travel convenient, we are operating a support center where the operators speak your native tongue and assist you with your enquiries. Whether you need help in a troubled situation or you just want to find a place near you to grab some decent dinner and relax, our operators are always there to assist you. We are here to provide answers to your questions and solutions to your problems. Forget about translators, Talk to a real person and get your queries answered in no time, that too; in your own language. We welcome you to Nepal.
